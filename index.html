ABOUT



This year's VCHS Student Council proposed to change two parts of the dress code on November 3, 2021. We aim to allow boys eventually to wear earrings and allow for hats in chapel based on individual preferences. Not only is our proposal a formal request of our student community, but it is also founded on strong Biblical basis as well as in consideration of its broader impacts, if passed.
